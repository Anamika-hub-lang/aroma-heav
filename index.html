Our Story

Founded in 2024, Aroma Haven started as a small coffee cart with a big dream. Our passion for exceptional coffee, commitment to quality, and love for our community has helped us grow into the welcoming space we are today.

We source our beans directly from sustainable farms around the world, ensuring both exceptional taste and ethical practices. Every cup we serve is crafted with care by our skilled baristas who are as passionate about coffee as you are.
